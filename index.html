About

My path to product wasn't linear. I started in sales at companies like Expedia and LivingSocial, where I learned to deeply understand customer needs. This foundation now shapes how I build product, always anchored in real user problems and measurable outcomes.

Today, I lead the product strategy for the largest real estate portfolio, serving millions of users. I specialize in platform consolidation, growth through SEO and GEO, experimentation, and building teams and systems that make great products sustainable.

Before all of this, I worked in non-profits and served as a Peace Corps volunteer in Uganda, an experience that taught me resourcefulness, cross-cultural collaboration, and the impact of thoughtful problem-solving.
